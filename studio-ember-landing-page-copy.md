## 7. The AI Part

**What we mean by AI agents**

# Software that does something.

We build agents around specific jobs inside your product or company.

### Support

Resolve common issues, retrieve information, take approved actions and escalate the cases that need a person.

### Onboarding

Guide users through setup, answer contextual questions and help them reach value faster.

### Workflows

Move data between tools, qualify requests, update records and complete repetitive internal tasks.

The rule is simple:

If it only talks, it is probably a chatbot.

If it can complete useful work, it is getting closer.
